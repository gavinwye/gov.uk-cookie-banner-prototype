Prove your identity to continue

Choose how you want to prove your identity so you can apply for a DBS basic check.

Use GOV.UK Verify

Registering with GOV.UK Verify usually takes about 15 minutes. It works best if you have:

  • a UK address
  • a valid passport or photocard driving licence

A certified identity provider will check your identity when you register with GOV.UK Verify. They’ve all met security standards set by government.
